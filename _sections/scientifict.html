--- title: Scientific icon: fa-flask order: 4 --- Math is the language of science so a machine must be able to understand math. So to make a machine understand math and execute it effectively, I worked with math and scientific libraries (Numpy, SciPy) and getting data to the math functions, be it raw/text (Pandas), images (OpenCV, Pillow), from the web via web-scrapping (scrapy, requests, beautiful-soup) or through RESTful APIs (JSON, XML). Additionally the data needs to be presented in a human understandable form through graph plotting (Matplotlib, seaborn) along with explanations(Jupyter notebooks).
